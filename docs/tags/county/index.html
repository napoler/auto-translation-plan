Lanzhou: Injured peregrine falcon settles in wildlife rescue station

Posted on December 1, 2020

Original title: Warm-hearted citizens timely rescued the injured peregrine falcon and made it “home” in the wildlife rescue station. Injured peregrine falcon. On November 26, Mr. Wang, a citizen, reported that he and his colleagues found an injured peregrine falcon on the rooftop of the Asia-Europe International (AEI) building, and they carried it back to the building and put it into a small cardboard box. In the afternoon, the injured peregrine falcon was accepted by the Gansu Lanzhou Wildlife Rescue Station. [Read More]
